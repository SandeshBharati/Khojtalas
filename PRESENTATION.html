Introduction

What is KhojTalas?

KhojTalas (Nepali: "search for it") is Nepal's dedicated lost & found web platform that connects people who've lost their belongings with those who've found them — powered by AI matching technology.

📝 Report lost or found items with rich details
🤖 AI matches items using text, image, location & time
🔔 Instant notifications when a match is found
🇳🇵 Covers all 77 districts & 400+ municipalities
🔍 📍 📦 🔔

The Problem

Why Do Lost Items Stay Lost?

😰

No Dedicated Platform

Nepal lacks a centralized, organized platform for reporting and finding lost items. People are left on their own.

📱

Social Media Limitations

Facebook and TikTok posts get buried in feeds, have no search filters, and reach is algorithm-dependent.

🤷

Low Reporting Motivation

Finders have little incentive to report items they've found — no reward system, no easy process.

🔓

Trust & Scam Risks

No verification process means anyone can claim ownership — leading to scams and false claims.

Intelligence Core

AI-Powered Matching

Our multi-factor AI algorithm combines four independent scoring dimensions to produce high-confidence matches — only notifying users at ≥95% confidence.

📝
Text Similarity (NLP)
384-dim embeddings via all-MiniLM-L6-v2 + cosine similarity
🖼️
Image Similarity (pHash)
Perceptual hashing — compares visual fingerprints across all photo pairs
📍
Location Scoring (Haversine)
Great-circle distance: <1km = 100%, 1–5km = linear decay, >20km = 0%
⏰
Time Window Analysis
Scores overlap between when item was lost and when it was found
